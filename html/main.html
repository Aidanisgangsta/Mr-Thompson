Here are my top 5 printers

1 - Canon Pixma TS9120

I give this printer a 6/10.
Don't get me wrong, this is a beautiful printer which produces amazing quality prints. However, it has a slow printing speed and only holds 100 sheets of paper meaning it is not as good as it could be.

2 - Riso ComColor GD9630

I give this printer a 10/10.
This printer is the pinnacle of human innovation. This printer prints at a blistering 160 pages per minute while maintaining beautiful print quality. I own 5 of these printers in my office and it is the best use of money ever. Being able to print over 13 pages a second has allowed my printing to bypass what was previously thought to be possible.
